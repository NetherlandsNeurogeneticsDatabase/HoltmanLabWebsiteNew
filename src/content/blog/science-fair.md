Our annual Science Fair takes place on May 2nd. Students in grades 3–5 are encouraged to submit projects. Sign-up sheets are in the main office.

Projects can cover any scientific topic of the student's choosing. Teachers will be available during lunch hours to help with ideas and guidance. We can't wait to see the creativity our young inventors bring this year!
