We are excited to share a new publication in collaboration with Karim Kreft (University of Nottingham) on the genetics of Multiple Sclerosis.

In a large MS patient cohort, three distinct genetic subtypes of MS were identified. These findings provide new insights into the heterogeneity of the disease.
