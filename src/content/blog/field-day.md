Save the date! Field Day is May 16th. Students will enjoy relay races, tug-of-war, face painting, and more. Parent volunteers are welcome!

Field Day is one of the most beloved events of our school year. If you'd like to volunteer, please contact the front office or sign up via the school portal. Wear comfortable clothes and bring plenty of school spirit!
